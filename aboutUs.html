About Us

Fosua Hotel, located in Kumasi, approximately 1.9 kilometres of Baba Yara Sport Stadium, provides a comfortable stay in the city center. The 16-room deluxe hotel was created with comfort, business promotion, and leisure in mind, as well as outstanding cuisine. The hotel is about a 15-minute drive from the airport and a short walk from the central shopping district. Whether you are traveling for business, romance, or holiday, we are without a doubt the best option. For visitors that drive, the Fosua Hotel offers accommodations with a terrace as well as complimentary private parking. A restaurant, a 24-hour front desk, and room service, as well as complimentary Wi-Fi, are available at this accommodation. Each morning, the accommodation serves a full English/Irish breakfast. The facility includes a hair salon and a clinic.
